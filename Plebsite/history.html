Hannibal of Carthage

This man has got to be one of my favorite generals of all time. With less men, constant attrition, and the mighty Roman boot after him, he beat down Rome hard enough that the romans just couldn't face him head on. In his most succesful victory, the battle of Cannae, The kill-death ratio was 10:1.
He is the one of if not the greatest military general of all time and fascinates strategist to this day.
