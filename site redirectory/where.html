We are missing Japan-the birthplace of all that is Pokemon? The developers want to fix these problems before releasing it to the rest of the world for all to enjoy.

Pokestops

Pokestops are an esential part of the game as visiting one will give the player extra pokeballs along with one or two additional items. Pokestops can be found in stores, restaurants, colleges, parks, and surprisingly, graveyards. The cities and colleges are brimming with pokestops and gyms in every corner and result in even the restaurants taking advantage of the boom to help there business as well.

Pokemon Locations?

Pokemon will tend to spawn in what would be in there native habitat. For instance, water-type pokemon tend to spawn near lakes and the ocean while desert-not so much. Although, some common pokemon seem to appear everywhere you go, such as weedle and ratata.
